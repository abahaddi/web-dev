The Best Movies According to Amin

My Top 3 Movies of All Time 🎬

13 Hours

I like 13 Hours because it is based on a true story and shows realistic military action with a lot of tension and teamwork.

Lone Survivor

I like Lone Survivor because the story is emotional, intense, and shows courage and survival in difficult situations.

The Man from U.N.C.L.E

I like this movie because it mixes action, comedy, and spy missions with a cool style and fun characters.
